About Me

I am a former art major turned engineering major turned computer science major graduated with a B.S. from UC Irvine in Spring of 2020! I love animation so I wanted to become an animator, but I am a huge nerd and I love physics and math so I switched to mechanical engineering. But then, I found computer science and I fell in love with it. CS now allows me to go into the field of animation.


My goal is to work in the animation field as a technical director, combining my love for art, storytelling and computer science. In the meantime, I'm continuing to learn and improve my skills in technical art. I love the work artists do in animation and I would love to be able to make their work easier!
